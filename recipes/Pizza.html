Pepperoni and Ham Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is an American pizza variety which includes one of the country's most beloved toppings. Pepperoni is actually a corrupted form of peperoni (one “p”), which denotes a large pepper in Italian, but nowadays it denotes a spicy salami, usually made with a mixture of beef, pork, and spices. This, combined with the delicious taste of Ham creates a symphony of flavor that is not healthy, but absolutley delicious!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano
  • Tomato
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Sliced Ham

Optional Toppings

  • Granulated Parmesan Cheese
  • Crushed Red Pepper
