Filet Mignon

Description

Filet mignon is a lean and delicious meat cut from the heart of the tenderloin, making it one of the most desirable cuts of steak. It is arguably one of the most tender cuts because the tenderloin, a muscle that does virtually nothing, rarely gets toughened.

Ingredients

  • Extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • Filet Mignon
  • Kosher Salt
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Butter
  • Rosemary

Optional Sides

  • Garlic Mashed Potatoes
  • Brussel Sprouts
  • Caramelized Carrots
